About Terra Hill

Mogg's Terra Hill is a sprawling 40-acre green managed farmland located near Bengaluru-Mangaluru Expressway, just 45 minutes drive from Nelamangala at KUNIGAL. Consist of 100+ Plots. Surrounded by lush mountains and dense plantations, it offers a serene escape into nature.

Featuring a 4-acre water stream with advanced drip irrigation, this property is perfect for sustainable agriculture or investment. It's modern amenities, including a clubhouse, swimming pool, children's play area, and a temple, etc. make it a harmonious blend of nature and luxury.

Terra Hill isn't just land; it's a gateway to peace, growth, and opportunity.
