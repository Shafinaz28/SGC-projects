A Cultivated Farm Experience

The Aira farms Phase 2 is a cultivated and productive land with various fruit and forest bearing trees that would add to immediate revenues on a 7 acre land area.

Located around 50 kilometers from Bangalore, the region boasts a green ambiance with a stunning hill vista right in front of it. To secure pleasant stay at the farm, we have set up a variety of facilities including fencing, lighting, restrooms, and a security house. The location is ideal for you and your family to spend time in nature and gain from nature's assets.
