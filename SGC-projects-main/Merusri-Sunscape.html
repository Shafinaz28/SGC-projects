A Gated Community of Serenity

Merusri Sunscape is premium boutique villa residences spread across 5.39 acres at IVC Road, Bengaluru. With just 76 boutique 3 & 4 bed villas, the project offers exclusivity, efficient layouts, and lush green surroundings.

Designed for openness, ventilation, and community living, it features a 12,000 sq.ft clubhouse, landscaped greens, coworking pods, sports zones, and a pet park. Built with sustainable materials, Sunscape blends natural serenity with everyday convenience — redefining villa living in North Bengaluru.
